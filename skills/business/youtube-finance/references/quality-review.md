# Video Quality Review Checklist — Global Signal

## SCORING
- 35-40/40: POST IMMEDIATELY
- 28-34: MINOR FIXES needed
- 20-27: MAJOR REVISION required
- Below 20: REMAKE recommended

## SECTION 1: TECHNICAL (Max 10)
- Resolution 1080p or higher
- Audio clear, no echo/noise
- Voiceover synced correctly
- No visual glitches
- Correct 16:9 aspect ratio
- Smooth transitions only

## SECTION 2: CONTENT (Max 10)
- Hook strong in first 30 seconds
- No "In this video..." opener
- Conversational not robotic tone
- Specific facts with numbers/dates
- Economic impact clearly explained
- New revelation every 3-4 minutes
- Call to action at end

## SECTION 3: SEO (Max 10)
- Thumbnail high contrast readable
- Title has power word + keyword
- Description 200+ words
- Tags include trending terms
- Chapters added
- Subscribe reminder shown
- End screen last 20 seconds
- Educational disclaimer added

## SECTION 4: VIRAL POTENTIAL (Max 10)
- Topic trending last 48 hours
- Emotional trigger in first 10 seconds
- Competitor videos performing well
- Unique angle not covered elsewhere
- Title creates curiosity gap
- Thumbnail stops the scroll

## COMMON FIXES
- Weak hook: Rewrite opening 30 seconds with most shocking fact
- Poor audio: Re-render ElevenLabs at higher quality
- Bad thumbnail: Generate new with Higgsfield dark background + red arrows
- No economic impact: Add 3-4 minute section connecting to markets/money
- No CTA: Add 30-second subscribe outro
- Too short: 18min=2400 words, 20min=2700 words, 25min=3300 words
